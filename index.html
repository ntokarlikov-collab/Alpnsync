The Alpine Logistics Nightmare

Traditional travel apps look for cheap hotels. They don't know if a mountain pass is closed due to a 45 km/h blizzard or if an avalanche hazard tier just spiked.

Volatile Safety Risks

Avalanche risks change hourly. A route at 2,500 meters that was safe on Thursday can become entirely lethal by Saturday morning.

Fragmented Transit Mazes

Matching cross-border FlixBus schedules with regional mountain rail lines while transporting 20 kg of heavy winter gear.

Hut & Ticket Scarcity

Manually checking remote mountain hut (refugio) seasonal openings against variable student group discount structures.

The Oxygen Test

Why this requires a highly integrated AI engine

If you remove the AI, this system collapses. A basic database filter cannot cross-reference European Avalanche Warning Services (EAWS) safety logs with multi-modal train tracking loops simultaneously.

Multi-Stream Ingestion: Continuous parsing of weather models, transit timelines, and terrain safety indexes.

Human-In-The-Loop: The Student Trip Leader acts as the ultimate authority, validating group gear competencies and adjusting safety constraints.
